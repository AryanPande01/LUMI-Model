Step 13: Training Improvements

Initial training:

MSE Loss only

Problem:

IC ≈ 0
Rank IC ≈ 0

Model minimized error but learned poor stock ranking.









Step 14: Correlation-Aware Objective

Implemented:

loss =
MSE
+
0.1 × IC_Loss

Purpose:

Encourage predictions to follow the correct market ranking.
